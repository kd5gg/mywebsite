Kd5gg Hamradio Station

Richard McDanial was my Elmer. He was retired from Radio Stack. My introduction into Hamradio was with a handheld scanner. Texas had a link system called the southwest link system. From my house I could hear a local ham named Richard and he was in Boerne, Tx. I googled his address and knocked on the door. In 2005 I got my ham ticket. Richard made a deal with me if I get my code endorsement he would give me a radio. I studied for 6 months and I got my code endorsement. I was given a HTX10.
